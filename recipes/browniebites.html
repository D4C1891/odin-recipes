Brownie Bites

Description

Brownie Bites are a bite size version of the traditional, homemade fudgy brownies we crave! They are perfectly chocolatey, deliciously buttery, and make a great base for a mini swirl of luscious frosting.

Ingredients

  • Unsalted Butter
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • All-purpose Flour
  • Dutch Process Cocoa Powder
  • Mini Semi Sweet Chocolate Chips

How to make Brownie Bites

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Spray a 24 cup mini muffin pan with non-stick cooking Spray and dust with 2 tsp cocoa powder. Shake to coat evenly and set aside.
  3. Place butter in a large microwave safe mixing bowl. Heat butter until just melted in microwave.
  4. Whisk in sugar and salt. Then whisk in eggs and vanilla extract, blending well.
  5. Add flour and cocoa powder and stir mixture until combined, while scraping sides and bottom of bowl.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips
  7. Scoop batter out about 1.5 Tbsp at a time and drop into prepared muffin pan filling until nearly full.
  8. Bake in preheated oven until just set, about 15 to 17 minutes.
  9. Let cool in pan 15 minutes before transferring brownie bites to a wire rack to finish cooling. If desired, top with frosting.
